# Impeccable Design Context — VM Portfolio

## Design Context

### Users
Hiring managers, senior PM recruiters, potential collaborators. Browsing on desktop during work hours. Evaluating Varun as a person and professional — they want to feel like they're meeting someone, not reading a resume.

### Brand Personality
**Human, curious, builder.** Warm but substantive. Someone you'd want to work with. Not flashy, not corporate. The kind of person who ships things and thinks carefully about why they matter.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light theme. Editorial but grounded — not a design portfolio, not a corporate one-pager. Confident without being loud. The photo is a hero element: it should make you feel like you're meeting Varun, not just reading about him. Typography should feel considered but approachable — avoid anything that signals "I hired a designer." Avoid AI slop aesthetics: no gradient text, no glassmorphism, no floating metric cards.

### Design Principles
1. **The person first** — Varun's photo and voice should lead. Copy and credentials support, never dominate.
2. **Earn every word** — Dense paragraphs are a sign of unedited thinking. Short, specific sentences only.
3. **No decoration for decoration's sake** — If a visual element doesn't carry meaning, remove it.
4. **Light but not bland** — Light theme with genuine contrast and warmth, not white + gray + blue.
5. **Built, not generated** — The interface should feel like a person made intentional choices, not like a template was filled in.
